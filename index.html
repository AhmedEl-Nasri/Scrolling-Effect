The Neon Melody......... Ava Lane danced in the hidden tunnels beneath Liora, the neon lights and electric graffiti lighting her path. The city thrummed with music, and she moved with every beat, her body alive to the rhythm.

Tonight's performance was risky. The SynthGuard drones patrolled the skies, and Ava knew they wouldn't tolerate rebellion. Her partner, Dex, stood nearby, his voice calm through her comms: “Keep dancing. Move toward the exit.”

The beat dropped, and Ava spun, her movements sharp and fluid. She could feel the music pulling her, but then—red lights. A SynthGuard drone locked onto her. “Run,” Dex said.

Ava sprinted toward the escape hatch, her breath sharp. Dex activated counter-holograms, creating distractions behind her. The drone’s light grew closer, but they made it out. They collapsed into the shadows, breathless. “That was close,” Ava said. Dex grinned. “But they felt the music.”

Ava smiled. Freedom was a melody, and she would always keep dancing.
